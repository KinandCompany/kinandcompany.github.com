## Photobooth 1
A custom photobooth to capture, print and store images. The unique detailing and patterning draw the viewer into the camera’s eye. A touch screen controls all the booth’s functionality. The booth is a modular system, allowing for ease of assembly and transport.

Brake formed steel sheet metal with semi-gloss white, grey and mint powder-coat.
